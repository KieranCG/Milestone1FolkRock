Biography

Adam

Adam is the lead singer and songwriter of Green Fields. He is a gifted vocalist with a powerful and expressive range. He is also a talented songwriter, and his lyrics are often both personal and universal.

Daniel

Daniel is the bassist of Green Fields. He is a solid foundation for the band, and he provides a deep, rich sound. He is also a talented vocalist, and he often sings harmony vocals on the band's songs.

Gloria

Gloria is the drummer of Green Fields but also transitions to the cello. She is a powerhouse behind the kit, and she provides the band with a driving beat. She is also a dynamic performer, and she often adds her own personal flair to the band's live shows.

Peter

Peter is the guitarist of Green Fields. He is a skilled musician with a keen ear for melody. He is also a creative improviser, and he often adds unexpected twists and turns to the band's songs.
