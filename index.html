Bio

Green Fields are a folk rock wedding band that specializes in creating a fun and festive atmosphere for your special day. We are a premium, customizable band that can accommodate groups of any size. Our repertoire includes a wide variety of folk rock songs, as well as popular hits from all genres. We are passionate about creating a memorable experience for our clients, and we pride ourselves on filling dance floors. If you are looking for a band that will bring energy and excitement to your wedding, Green Fields is the perfect choice for you. We would be honored to be a part of your big day.

Features

History

The Green Fields are an Irish folk rock band from Dublin. The band was formed in 2010 by four friends who shared a love of traditional Irish music and rock and roll. The band's name is a reference to the green fields of Ireland, which are a source of inspiration for their music. Green Fields have released two albums, "The Green Fields" (2012) and "The Wild Rover" (2015). Their music has been praised for its beautiful melodies, catchy lyrics, and energetic live performances. The band has toured extensively throughout Ireland and the United Kingdom, and they have shared the stage with some of the biggest names in Irish folk music, including The Dubliners, The Chieftains, and Altan.
